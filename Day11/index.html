Our Most Visited Places

Most Visited Tourist Places in Karnataka
  • Mysore Palace, Mysuru

    • The grand royal palace known for its stunning architecture and illuminated nights during festivals.

  • Bengaluru (Bangalore) City Attractions

    • Includes Lalbagh Botanical Garden, Cubbon Park, Bangalore Palace, and vibrant markets.

  • Hampi

    • Famous for its UNESCO World Heritage ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire.

  • Coorg (Kodagu)

    • Popular hill station known for coffee plantations, scenic landscapes, and waterfalls.

  • Gokarna Beaches

    • Pristine, less commercialized beaches attracting both pilgrims and beach lovers.

  • Shravanabelagola

    • The pilgrimage site with the huge statue of Lord Bahubali draws thousands, especially during the Mahamastakabhisheka festival.

  • Badami Caves

    • Rock-cut cave temples with exquisite carvings, part of the Chalukya dynasty heritage.

  • Bandipur National Park

    • A major wildlife sanctuary known for tiger and elephant sightings.

  • Kabini Wildlife Sanctuary

    • Another hotspot for wildlife enthusiasts with rich flora and fauna.

  • Chikmagalur

    • Coffee capital of Karnataka, famous for trekking and serene coffee estates.
